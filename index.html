AlarmDroid

Time to get up!

The powerful, yet simple alarm clock app for Android.

The new version 2.0 brings the new Material Design, plenty of new themes, a beautiful new time picker, and overall performance improvements.

"Ad-free" add-on

The development of this Android app is supported by in-app advertisements powered by Google AdMob. If you are generally not comfortable with ads, you can purchase the "ad-free" addon in the app (as of version 2.0).

In AlarmDroid, go to Menu > About, and hit the "Purchase ad-free add-on" button.

Get Support
